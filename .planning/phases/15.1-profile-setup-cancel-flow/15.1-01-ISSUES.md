### UAT-001: Back button navigation animation slides from wrong direction

**Discovered:** 2026-02-02
**Resolved:** 2026-02-02 - Inline fix during UAT
**Phase/Plan:** 15.1-01
**Severity:** Minor
**Feature:** Cancel profile setup navigation
**Description:** When pressing the back arrow on ProfileSetupScreen, the phone number screen slides in from the right. It should slide in from the left to match standard iOS "back" navigation animation.
**Expected:** Phone number screen slides in from the left (standard back navigation)
**Actual:** Phone number screen slides in from the right
**Fix:** Added `animation: 'slide_from_left'` to PhoneInput screen options in AppNavigator.js
