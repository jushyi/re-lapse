<task type="checkpoint:human-verify" gate="blocking">
  <what-built>Album creation and management</what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
    **Step 1: Create Album**
    1. Go to Profile → tap "+" or "Create Album" button
    2. Verify CreateAlbumScreen appears
    3. Enter album title (e.g., "Test Album")
    4. Tap "Select Photos"
    5. Select 5-10 photos from your profile photos
    6. Verify selected photos show checkmarks or badges
    7. Tap "Next" or "Done"
    8. Choose a cover photo from the selected photos
    9. Tap "Create Album"
    10. Verify returns to profile with new album visible

    **Expected:**
    - Album creation flow is intuitive
    - Photo selection allows multi-select
    - Cover photo selection required
    - Album appears in profile album section immediately

    **Step 2: View Album**
    1. From profile, tap the newly created album
    2. Verify AlbumGridScreen opens
    3. Verify all selected photos appear in grid
    4. Verify cover photo displayed prominently (or as first photo)
    5. Tap a photo to open detail view
    6. Swipe between photos
    7. Verify can navigate all photos in album

    **Expected:**
    - Album grid loads all photos
    - Photo detail modal works within album context
    - Swipe navigation constrained to album photos

    **Step 3: Edit Album**
    1. From album grid, tap menu (three dots) → "Edit Album"
    2. Verify can edit album title
    3. Change title to "Edited Album"
    4. Tap "Add Photos"
    5. Select 2 more photos
    6. Tap "Save"
    7. Verify album grid updates with new photos
    8. Verify title changed

    **Expected:**
    - Album title editable
    - Photos can be added after creation
    - Changes persist immediately

    **Step 4: Remove Photos from Album**
    1. From album grid in edit mode, tap "Remove" on a photo
    2. Verify confirmation or immediate removal
    3. Verify photo removed from album (but still in profile)
    4. Exit edit mode
    5. Verify photo no longer in album

    **Expected:**
    - Photos removable from album
    - Original photo remains in profile grid
    - Album updates correctly

    **Step 5: Delete Album**
    1. From album grid, tap menu → "Delete Album"
    2. Verify confirmation alert
    3. Tap "Delete"
    4. Verify returns to profile
    5. Verify album removed from profile album section
    6. Go to profile grid → verify photos still exist (album delete doesn't delete photos)

    **Expected:**
    - Album deletion requires confirmation
    - Album removed from profile
    - Photos preserved in profile

<task type="checkpoint:human-verify" gate="blocking">
  <what-built>Monthly albums and Selects banner</what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
    **Step 1: Monthly Albums**
    1. Go to Profile → Albums section
    2. Look for monthly albums (e.g., "February 2026", "January 2026")
    3. Verify at least one monthly album exists (created automatically from revealed photos)
    4. Tap a monthly album
    5. Verify photos from that month appear
    6. Verify album is read-only or has limited edit options (automatic albums may not be editable)

    **Expected:**
    - Monthly albums auto-created for months with photos
    - Album title format is consistent (e.g., "Month YYYY")
    - Photos grouped correctly by month

    **If no monthly albums exist:**
    This may indicate photos are too recent or feature not enabled. Note it and move on.

    **Step 2: Selects Banner**
    1. Go to Profile (main profile screen)
    2. Verify Selects banner appears at top (3 featured photos in horizontal row)
    3. If empty (new account), tap "+" to set selects
    4. Select 3 photos from profile
    5. Verify selected photos appear in banner
    6. Tap a photo in the banner
    7. Verify photo opens in detail view

    **Expected:**
    - Selects banner visible on profile
    - Can select/change featured photos
    - Tapping banner photo opens detail view

    **Step 3: Edit Selects**
    1. From profile, tap edit icon on Selects banner (or long-press)
    2. Verify can deselect and reselect photos
    3. Change selects to 3 different photos
    4. Save changes
    5. Verify banner updates with new photos

    **Expected:**
    - Selects are editable
    - Changes reflect immediately
    - Exactly 3 photos required

    **Step 4: Selects Reordering (if feature exists)**
    1. In edit mode, drag photos to reorder
    2. Verify order updates
    3. Save
    4. Verify banner shows photos in new order

    **Expected:**
    - Drag-to-reorder works smoothly (from Phase 4.1)
    - Order persists after save

    **If reordering doesn't exist:** Note it and move on.
