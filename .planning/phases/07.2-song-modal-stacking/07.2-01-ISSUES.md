### UAT-001: Clip selection modal should fade in, not slide in

**Discovered:** 2026-01-28
**Resolved:** 2026-01-28
**Phase/Plan:** 07.2-01
**Severity:** Cosmetic
**Feature:** Clip selection overlay
**Description:** The black transparent overlay that appears with the clip preview slides in instead of fading in. User prefers fade-in animation.
